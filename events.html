GraceLand Missionary Church

These are what we stand for

Marriage And Family Ministry

This ministry provides Godly guidance, counseling and mentorship that builds healthy marriage and family. If you are planning to get married, Graceland offers pre-marital program that gives couples effective Godly tools enabling your marriage to be a lasting and fulfilling one. This ministry also provides inspiration, resources, and support to parents in their parenting journey. Classes offered opens parents’ eyes to biblical parenting principles and how to develop a clear game plan for righteously raising your child or children to maturity.

Prison, Hospital and Homeless Ministry

Graceland is passionate about Jesus’ command, “Go into the world and preach the gospel to all creation.” (Mk 16:15). We believe in the tangible expression of God love which is rooted in compassion for those who are forgotten and hurting, love in action opens doors for the gospel. It is a lifestyle of the church to extend the gospel and love to the homeless, those in prisons and in hospitals.

Men and Women Ministry

This ministry offers opportunities for men and women to gather for prayer and fellowship in various settings. We seek to develop strong leaders according to Proverbs 27:17 “Iron sharpens iron”, by sharing information and experiences that help them to grow spiritually as matured Christians. The ministry meetings include prayer breakfasts, evening bible studies, leadership series, and fun moments.

Social Wellfare and Refuge Ministry

Graceland’s welcoming team encourages its members to respond to the needs of the refugees, asylees and forcibly displaced persons, and support the efforts of partner agencies that resettles refugees and asylees in the United States of America. We are dedicated to put in place activities and programs that help ease the journey of families eager to start their new life away from violence and fear. Our activities and programs include but are not limited to the provision of pro-bono legal aid services, psycho-social assistance, educational program for children, food donation, as well as poverty alleviation and job placement initiatives.

Children and Youth Ministry

Our desire is that children and youth will experience the excitement and power of God whenever they come to church, by creating an atmosphere that is nurturing and loving. Here at Graceland, children will not only have fun playing with their friends, but also by learning how to apply age appropriate scripture lessons to their daily lives. In the midst of the chaos of adolescence, we walk faithfully with our youth as they transition from children to young adult by helping them grow lasting relationship with Jesus Christ, for they are tomorrow leaders.
